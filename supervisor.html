By following this SOP, supervisors can enhance customer satisfaction, foster team accountability and uphold organisational quality.

Bulk send notifications

  1. Click the Bulk Send notification button.
  2. Choose the appropriate option for sending the notification:
    1. If "All Users" is selected, the notification will be sent to everyone.

  4. Type your message in the Message box.

Change ringer

This will enable the functionality to change the ringing destination. If there is a headset plugged into the computer, it is easy to miss a call due to the device ringing on the headset. Changing the ringer will allow a call to ring on the computer’s physical speaker even with a headset plugged in.

  1. Click the change ringer button.
  2. Choose the appropriate option for selecting the ringer output:
    1. If "Default" is selected, the system will use the default audio device.
    2. If "Communications Default" is selected, this will be used for audio related to communication apps (e.g., calls, meetings).
    3. If "Speakers" is selected, audio will be played through connected speakers or headphones using the Realtek audio driver.
    4. If “Headphones” are selected, the device will ring through the connected headphones.

Step 1: Click the change ringer button.

Step 2: Choose between default, communications default, speakers or Headphones.

Queue

The virtual line where incoming interactions or customer requests are placed (calls, emails, messages) while waiting to be handled by available agents.

  1. Click the "Go on Queue" Button: Select the "Go on Queue" button to become available for incoming requests and join the active interaction queue.
  2. Click the "Go off Queue" Button: When you need to stop receiving requests, click the "Go Off Queue" button to exit the call queue and become unavailable for further calls.

Click the "Go on Queue" Button: This button will turn into a green “Play” button to indicate that the user is on queue.

Click the "Go off Queue" Button: This button will turn into a red “Pause” button to indicate that the user is off queue.

Telephone

  1. Click the Telephone Button to prompt the voice calling function.
  2. Keypad Tab to dial the required phone number directly.
    1. Type the number by.
      1. Clicking the Keypad buttons.
      2. Typing in the text box.
    2. Click the dial button.
  3. User Tab to make a call to any other internal user within the organisation.
    1. Search for the user by:
      1. Typing the user’s name in the textbox.
      2. Scrolling through the list of users.
    2. Click the dial button.
  4. Department Tab to call any other internal department.
    1. Search for the department by:
      1. Typing the department name in the textbox.
      2. Scrolling through the list of departments.
    2. Click the dial button.
  5. Voicemail Tab to listen to and manage any voicemails that may have been received.
    1. Voicemails are indicated by a red "cassette" icon displayed over the telephone icon.
    2. Access the voicemail tab to view the list of voicemails that have been received.
    3. Action the voicemail.
      1. Listen: Play the voicemail to hear the message.
      2. Callback: Select the callback option to return the call to the voicemail sender.
      3. Delete: Choose to delete the voicemail if it is no longer needed.
  6. Test Audio Tab to check the audio quality before making a call, ensuring clear communication.
    1. Click the test audio button.
    2. Follow the voice commands for further instructions.
